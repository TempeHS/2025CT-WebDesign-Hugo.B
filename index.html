What Makes Something Timeless?


There are many things in the world that everyone knows of to some degree, like the works of Shakespeare, that have remained relevant for hundreds of years after his death, or famous videogames like Tetris or Mario. But what makes these things remain so popular and well known? This website will address a few points of criteria that I view as the things that make something timeless.







What Does Timeless Mean?


Something being timeless generally means that a substantial amount of people know of it. A good example of this is religion, which has been important to humans for a very long time, with many people not only knowing of it but believing in it and using it as a moral system.



What Makes Something Timeless: Criteria


- Having a significant affect on human culture


- Creating a viable and stable career option


- Being well known for over 100 years
